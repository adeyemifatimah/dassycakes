OUR VISION

To become one of the most sort after baker in the whole world and put a smile on peoples face
DassyisCakes was founded in 2016 when Dasola Amudo turned her passion for baking into a business. While in management consulting, she decided it was time to leave a job she liked and face the life she loves. The business was formerly known as Dassyis Confectioneries specializing in cookies, brownies, and muffins. Customers fell in love, word got out and requests for cakes started rolling in.


OUR MISSION

To create awareness about how you can achieve anything you want to achieve.
To be the first choice of consumers in Africa and create a benchmark in quality standards in the bakery industry.
